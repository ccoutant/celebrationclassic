Registration

Start your online registration by filling out this form. If you would prefer to download and print a registration form to mail in with your payment, you can download one of the registration forms listed at the bottom of this page.

You can register online in three easy steps:

  1. Provide your contact information, select a sponsorship level (if desired), and enter the number of golfers and dinner guests.

  2. Make your payment online through Acceptiva.

  3. After paying, you will be returned to this site where you will be asked to provide golfer information and make your dinner selections. After your payment is made, you can complete your registration at any time until April 26.

Current prices for golf and dinner are good through the regular registration deadline of April 16. Prices will be higher for late registration from April 17–26.

YOUR REGISTRATION MUST BE COMPLETED BY APRIL 26, 2012.

All proceeds from the Celebration Classic Dinner and Golf Tournament will benefit Congregation Shir Hadash and its community programs, which are aimed at improving the lives of many under-served populations in Santa Clara County. These include the annual Healthy Living Fair, which provides a wide array of health services and screenings at no charge; participation in interfaith Habitat for Humanity; non-sectarian support for our community’s elderly; providing food and supplies to local shelters, and school supplies to local children in need; the Early Childhood Center, which is open to all; and the new Job Board.
