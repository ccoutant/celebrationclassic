All proceeds from the Celebration Classic Dinner and Golf Tournament will benefit Congregation Shir Hadash and its community programs, which are aimed at improving the lives of many under-served populations in Santa Clara County. These include the annual Healthy Living Fair, which provides a wide array of health services and screenings at no charge; participation in interfaith Habitat for Humanity; non-sectarian support for our community’s elderly; providing food and supplies to local shelters, and school supplies to local children in need; the Early Childhood Center, which is open to all; and the new Job Board.
